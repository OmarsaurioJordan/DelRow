Ngubani ongathandi iphunga lesinkwa esishisayo, noma yikuphi ukudla okusanda kwenziwa, ngoba ukuvusa lowo muzwa kungumsebenzi wale ndima, futhi kulungile, futhi ukondla ... uphethe ukuguqula isivuno sibe yisibiliboco esinomsoco, okukhethekile kwakhe ngu: i-oats enesinamoni, ikhekhe likabhanana, i-arepa yommbila, i-burger kabhontshisi, amakhukhi e-quinoa, i-dessert chocolate, ikhekhe lezithelo elomisiwe Yondla umlingani munye ngesikhathi, okungukuthi, isikhathi ngasinye lapho enza ikhono lakhe elikhethekile; Amayunithi ondliwa ngokukhuthazwa kakhudlwana ukuhlasela ngokushesha okubanikeza ithuba; umphumela walokhu kudla okuhle uhlala isikhathi eside.

Njalo lapho kuvuselelwa ikhono lakhe elikhethekile, uzogqokisa iyunithi ehlangene ne-cape shockproof epholile, lokhu kunikeza izikhali zamayunithi ezigqitshwe ukushaywa okutholakele.

Lesi sakhamuzi esinamawala asizihluphi ngokushisa kwesisekelo, sihlala lapho siguqula amaminerali aluhlaza abe yizinto zobuciko zansuku zonke ezinjengalezi: amabhodwe, izingilazi, izisekelo, imigoqo, nazo zonke izinhlobo zensimbi ezisontekile; Phakathi kwabo ucelemba ngezikhathi ezithile, inkemba, imbazo yempi ... Njalo njalo udala isikhali esengeziwe futhi asinike umlingani, amayunithi alesi sikhali esingeziwe ahlaselwa kakhulu, kepha hhayi konke kuphelele, ngakho-ke kungekudala noma kamuva ngelinye ibhadi isikhali sizophuka.

Futhi-ke, ukuhlakanipha kokulingisa, baphulukisa ababambisene nabo, kungaba ukulimala ngokomzimba noma ukutheleleka ngegciwane.

Bekulokhu kukhona labo bantu abanezwi lokuyala, ukungasebenzi kahle ukwenza amaphrojekthi kanye nomuzwa oqinile wokuhlonishwa nokuzidela, le ndima inikeza emphakathini ikhono lokuqoqa nokuqondisa imibono, amaphrojekthi, izinhlelo, imibhikisho; Ukuze enze lokhu, kufanele alalele abanye, avule izinkundla zezingxoxo, abeke ukuhleleka futhi, okubaluleke kakhulu, abeke isibonelo. Ngaphandle kokuziphatha kwabanye ababambisene nabo, le ndima ibanxenxa ukuba bayilandele, ngaleyo ndlela bahlanganise iqembu elincane, ngale ndlela ngeke uhambe wedwa, enye inzuzo yalokhu ukuthi amalungu azovumelanisa lapho ephendula ukuhlaselwa komunye wabo .

Uyivelakancane, njengegwababa, onenhliziyo ecasukile futhi ejulile useke wabona imingcwabo eminingi, izinyembezi eziningi, iziphetho eziningi; Imvamisa ziyizikhungo ezizimele futhi ezizindlayo, indima yazo ukunikeza ukuphumula nokulala koshonile; ukufa, imfihlakalo enkulu empilweni, le ndima ihlale iphilwa futhi ilungele ukweluleka abalilayo. Linethempeli lapho linikezela khona ukuzinikela kwalo impilo yonke, leli thempeli lifanekisela unkulunkulu wabaningana ukuthi kukhona, abanye badala kunabanye, abanye bakhohliwe kakhulu kanti abanye banemfashini; ubheka isimilo somphakathi, akalale imithwalo yemizwa yakhe ngokuthembisa usizo oluphakeme. Angakwazi, ngaphansi kwengcindezi yombhikisho onokuthula, athathe indima yabo ezitheni, ebaphuca ikhono labo elikhethekile, lokhu ukwenza ngakunye isikhathi ngasinye lapho esebenzisa amandla akhe.

Ubudlelwano bomuntu nezinye izidalwa emvelweni budala, njengezilwane zamaphakethe, ukuzingela, ubungane, ukusetshenziswa; Le ndima ikhulisa izinja namagama futhi ibaqeqeshe, ukuze basebenze njengenkampani kuzo zonke izinhlobo zabantu, sonke siyazi ukuthi kukhona nalabo abababona njengamalungu omndeni.
